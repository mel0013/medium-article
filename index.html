What type of bread are you?

White Bread

You're more plain than a digestive biscuit. Congratulations! You're a lot more common. You're the equivalent of 0 negative blood type as the universal blood donor. You're the bread for everybody on whatever ocassion. People would eat you during the apocalypse.

Wholegrain

Are you as wholesome as your name? You're a wonder bread. So much fibre and healthy goodness packed into you. You may not be the first choice but you're definitely the healthiest. The constipated considers you a cure to their ailments. You're starting to give rise to a lot of healthy eaters out there. Don't stop pushing to the top of the sales catalogue!

Sourdough

You've risen from the depths of fermentation instead of yeast like your other bread cousins. You're sour, tangy and sassy. The good bacteria in our guts would like to thank you for feeding them.

Brioche

Fluffy, light and oh-so french~ Some say that the brioche has Romanian origins because it resembles to sweet holiday prepared in Romania. So much butter and eggs in you. You're a guilty pleasure and the lactose intolerants need to stay clear or eat you in small doses.
